Office of the Deputy Vice Chancellor (DVC)

I welcome you to the Office of the Deputy Vice Chancellor (DVC) at the Claretian University of Nigeria.

The Deputy Vice Chancellor is the head of Academic Division whose responsibilities are as follows:

  • Provision of strategic leadership for the academic function which includes the development of the academic portfolio and ensuring the implementation of innovative and successful strategies.
  • He also coordinates the examination process and ensures that timetable is followed accordingly.
  • Coordinating undergraduate and postgraduate studies.
  • He promotes the interests and values of the university internally and externally.
