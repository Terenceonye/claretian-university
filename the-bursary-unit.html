Welcome to CUN Bursary Unit

The Bursary unit at Claretian University of Nigeria is saddled with the following responsibilities:

  • Overseeing and managing the day-to-day financial transactions of the university.
  • Maintains studentâ€™s financial service payments record for all fees to ensure credibility and accuracy.
  • Manage annual budgets.
  • Prepares expense report.
  • Plans and develop strategies for generating resources or revenues for the university.
  • Ensuring that surplus funds are invested and managed properly.
  • Provides periodic reports of receipts and technical information to the university and to external funding agencies.
  • Interacts with internal and external auditors participating in auditing projects and provides information and access to accounting records required.

At CUN, the bursary unit is headed by a Bursar who is the Chief Executive Financial Officer of the university whose function is to record and advice the Vice Chancellor on day-to-day financial issues, relate with auditors and other supervising agents on financial matters.
