THE AUDIT VISUAL UNIT

This unit is set up to create collaborative and dynamic teaching and learning experience for CUN students through the provision of audio-visual aids which facilitate learning process and contribute to better understanding of subjects. Audio visual aids are being increasingly used because it involves both audio (verbal) as well as visual way of teaching to help students see better and have a simplified explanation of ideas.
