Welcome to CUN Senate Affairs Unit

The Senate is the academic engine of the university and it is responsible for the formulation of academic laws and policies that bounds the university. It is powered to act upon educational matters and controls academic activities.

Duties of the Senate:

  • Setting high level policy and strategy on learning, teaching and curriculum development.
  • They approve award of degrees and other qualifications in the university.
  • Maintaining the academic standards of the university award.
  • In charge of disciplining students.

In summary, Senate is the engine room of the university governing body where students, staff and administrators debate university issues and make conclusions on the policy and actions to be taken by the university.
