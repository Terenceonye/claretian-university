Undergraduate Degree Program

All prospective applicants seeking for Admission into Claretian University of Nigeria for the first year of any degree course must meet the following admission requirements process:

  • Applicant is required to have a minimum of 5 O-Level Credits including English Language and Mathematics in all courses.
  • The five credits should be obtained in not more than two sittings.
  • To apply for a programme, carefully read the admission requirement guidance and make sure you meet the criteria. Having met the criteria, click on the Apply Now button on the Home page.
  • Carefully fill in the details required correctly and click on Apply.
  • On successful submission, you are redirected to the page where you will get an invoice for form payment. Click on Pay online.
  • Next step is to enter your card details and click pay.
  • After a successful payment, you will receive payment application details through your email.

Print the application summary page that was sent to your email and bring it along during the screening/verification processes.
