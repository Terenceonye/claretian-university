BACHELOR OF SCIENCE (B. SC) IN PHYSICS WITH ELECTRONICS RENEWABLE ENERGY

BRIEF OVERVIEW

Bachelor of Science in Physics with Electronics Renewable Energy is an undergraduate degree awarded for completing a programme of study in the field of physics and its properties. This prepares students to have a very deep understanding of various aspects of fundamental knowledge of physics with Electronics. Students will gain practical background in all arrears of physics and advancing the field of electronics.

Claretian University of Nigeria have tailored and nurtured your studies to ensure you acquire basic skills essential for a successful career in this path which will be delivered by experienced teachers and specialists.

REQUIREMENTS

UTME ADMISSION

The entry requirements into the program shall be credit passes in five (5) subjects including English Language, Mathematics Physics, Chemistry and one other relevant subject to form the core subjects at SSCE/NECO/NABTEB in not more than 2 sittings.

Having met the admission requirements, applicants may proceed to apply for admission by clicking on Apply Now on the Admission Page on the website and carefully fill in the required information needed and select program of choice as Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) Physics with Electronics.
